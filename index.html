Who we are

About

Midwest Music Productions LLC is an independent music promotion company based in Lincoln, Nebraska. We exist for one reason: to give local artists real opportunities — real stages, real crowds, and real promotion across the Lincoln, Omaha, and surrounding region.

We don't care about industry politics. We care about the music. If you're making something worth hearing in the Midwest, we want to work with you. Whether you're booking your first show or building a following, we're here to help put you in front of the people who need to hear you.

Starting with our debut event — Punk in the Park Revival in Beatrice, NE on September 26th — we're building something worth being part of.
